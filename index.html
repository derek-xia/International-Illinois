This data visualization shows the change in enrollment of international students at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign from 1990 to 2018. Percentages are relative to total international student enrollment. Blue shades represent more male students than female students, and red shades represent more female students than male students. The area of the circle is proportional to the total enrollment.
